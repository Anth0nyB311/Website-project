Business Model

  • The main features of the well-being app are the live chat, the fitness and welfare points incentive. Our main source of income would be through subscriptions for the app that let users gain access to more fitness videos, a fast-track line to our live chats and a boost in the rate that people gain welfare points. We also gain revenue from ads that appear for users with the basic plan.

  • Another way we gain income is in partnerships with shop and restaurant chains, we charge for them to be featured on our app as an option where welfare points gift cards can be redeemed.
